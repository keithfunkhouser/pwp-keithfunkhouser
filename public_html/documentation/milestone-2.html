Content Strategy

My one page site will consist of the following sections

Introduction: This section will prominently display my name and a brief one-line description that includes the title Full Stack Web Developer, while also emphasizing my interest in UI/UX design. The background will be a somewhat abstract design referencing New Mexico/Southwestern art styles. Ideally this design will have a very subtle animation on user scroll.

Projects: This section will be an interactive showcase of around four projects that best represent my abilities as a web developer.

Skills: This section will emphasize the languages/frameworks that I am knowledgeable in through a responsive grid of icons and their associated names. It will also briefly highlight soft skills like my ability to learn quickly and work effectively in a team.

Contact: This section will list my professional email and include a simple contact form to easily get in touch with me. Below that will be icons/links to my LinkedIn and GitHub profiles.
