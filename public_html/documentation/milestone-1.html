PWP Milestone 1

Purpose, Audience & Goal

Purpose: This webpage will serve as a professional portfolio of my work as a full stack web developer, as well as be an example in itself of my abilities in frontend design.

Audience: This webpage will be targeted toward professionals in the web development / tech community of Albuquerque, NM. This includes business owners, hiring managers, recruiters and fellow developers.

Goal: The main goal of this webpage is to have those looking for talented developers reach out to me through the contact form. The page should immediately establish a unique style, experience and competency in web development.
